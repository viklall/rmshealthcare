📋 What You'll Need to Complete the Form

  1. Photo ID — Driver's license or other government-issued identification
  2. Insurance card(s) — Primary and any secondary insurance
  3. Current medications list — Names, doses, and how often you take them
  4. Previous doctor information — Name, address, phone, and fax of your prior physician
  5. Emergency contact — Name, relationship, and phone number
  6. Pharmacy information — Name and location of your preferred pharmacy

🏥 Before Your First Visit

  1. Arrive 15 minutes early to allow time for check-in and any remaining paperwork
  2. Bring your insurance card and photo ID — we need to make copies for your file
  3. Bring your co-payment — co-pays and existing balances are due prior to seeing the doctor
  4. Bring your completed intake form — whether downloaded, printed, or we'll provide one at the office
