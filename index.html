Meet Dr. Ashok Lall

Dr. Ashok Lall is an internal medicine specialist with more than 25 years of experience caring for patients in Santa Clarita. He completed his residency at Saginaw Cooperative Hospitals in Michigan after training at the Jewish Hospital in Cincinnati, where he also served as Chief Resident.

Since 1997, Dr. Lall has provided evidence-based, compassionate care for adults, helping patients stay healthy and manage chronic conditions. He is also a member of the medical staff at Henry Mayo Newhall Memorial Hospital.

At RMS Healthcare, we go beyond standard primary care. Through our program called Superior Care, patients receive the very latest in diagnostic, preventive, and personalized medical services.

Frequently Asked Questions

We want every patient to feel prepared and confident when visiting RMS Healthcare. Below are answers to some of the most common questions about our clinic and services.

What Should I Bring To My First Appointment?

Please bring your photo ID, insurance card, a list of current medications, and any recent test results or medical records you'd like Dr. Lall to review.

Do You Accept My Insurance?

We accept almost all PPO plans, Medicare and Medi-Cal. In addition, we are also providers for HealthCare Partners and many HMO plans. We recommend you check with your insurance carrier prior to making an appointment.

Can I Book A Virtual Appointment?

Yes — we offer telemedicine visits via Zoom or telephone for follow-ups, prescription refills, and consultations that don't require an in-person exam.
